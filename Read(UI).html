Our Design Process

Every project follows our proven 6-phase approach to ensure we deliver designs that are not only beautiful but also highly functional, user-friendly, and aligned with your business objectives.

1

Discovery & Research

I begin by understanding your business goals, target audience, and market landscape. This includes user research, competitor analysis, and defining key user personas.

2

Wireframing & Prototyping

I create low-fidelity wireframes to establish the information architecture, then develop interactive prototypes to test and refine the user flow before visual design begins.

3

Visual Design

Applying your brand identity, I craft pixel-perfect interfaces with attention to typography, color theory, and visual hierarchy to create an emotionally engaging experience.

4

User Testing

I conduct usability testing with real users to validate design decisions, identify pain points, and ensure the interface is intuitive before development begins.

5

Implementation Support

I provide developers with comprehensive design systems, style guides, and assets to ensure faithful implementation of the designs.

6

Iteration & Optimization

After launch, I analyze user behavior and feedback to recommend data-driven improvements that enhance conversion and satisfaction.

Discovery & Research Phase

Our Discovery & Research phase is the foundation of every successful design project. We employ a comprehensive approach to ensure we fully understand your business context and user needs:

  • Stakeholder Interviews: In-depth discussions with key decision-makers to align business objectives
  • User Personas: Development of detailed user profiles based on real data and research
  • Competitive Analysis: Evaluation of competitors' strengths and weaknesses in UX/UI
  • User Journey Mapping: Visualization of current user flows and pain points
  • Market Research: Analysis of industry trends and best practices

This phase typically takes 2-3 weeks depending on project scope, and results in a comprehensive Research Report that guides all subsequent design decisions.

Wireframing & Prototyping

Our Wireframing & Prototyping phase transforms research insights into tangible design concepts:

  • Low-Fidelity Wireframes: Basic layouts focusing on information architecture and user flow
  • Interactive Prototypes: Clickable models that simulate user interactions
  • User Flow Diagrams: Visual representations of critical pathways through your product
  • Iterative Testing: Early validation with stakeholders and potential users
  • Responsive Framework: Designs that adapt to all device sizes from the start

We use industry-standard tools like Figma, Sketch, and Adobe XD to create prototypes that are 80% of the UX with 20% of the effort, allowing for rapid iteration before visual design begins.

Visual Design Process

Our Visual Design phase brings your product to life with stunning aesthetics that support usability:

  • Design Systems: Comprehensive style guides for consistent branding
  • UI Components: Reusable elements that accelerate development
  • Microinteractions: Subtle animations that enhance user engagement
  • Accessibility Audits: Color contrast and readability testing
  • Pixel-Perfect Mockups: High-fidelity designs of all key screens

We don't just make things pretty - we create visual hierarchies that guide users naturally to their goals while strengthening your brand identity. Our designs are always backed by UX principles that drive conversions.

User Testing Methodology

Our User Testing phase validates designs with real users before development begins:

  • Usability Testing: Moderated sessions observing real users
  • A/B Testing: Comparative evaluation of design alternatives
  • Heatmaps & Analytics: Quantitative data on user behavior
  • Accessibility Testing: Ensuring compliance with WCAG standards
  • Feedback Synthesis: Turning observations into actionable insights

We typically conduct testing with 5-8 participants per user segment, as research shows this identifies 85% of usability issues. All findings are documented in a prioritized recommendations report.

Implementation Support

Our Implementation Support ensures designs are accurately translated into code:

  • Design Handoff Packages: Detailed specs for developers
  • Component Libraries: Reusable UI elements for consistent implementation
  • Developer Collaboration: Regular check-ins to answer questions
  • Quality Assurance: Design reviews during development
  • Responsive Testing: Ensuring perfect rendering across devices

We provide developers with all assets in multiple formats (SVG, PNG, PDF) along with interactive prototypes that demonstrate intended animations and transitions. Our support continues until launch to ensure pixel-perfect implementation.

Iteration & Optimization

Our Iteration & Optimization phase continues improving your product post-launch:

  • Analytics Review: Identifying pain points in user flows
  • Performance Metrics: Tracking conversion improvements
  • User Feedback Analysis: Incorporating real user suggestions
  • A/B Testing: Validating design variations
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular design sprints for enhancements

Digital products are never "done" - we recommend quarterly UX reviews to identify new optimization opportunities based on changing user behaviors and business goals. Many clients see conversion improvements of 10-30% from ongoing optimization.

User Satisfaction Metrics

We measure user satisfaction through multiple quantitative and qualitative metrics:

  • System Usability Scale (SUS): Industry-standard 10-item questionnaire (scores typically improve 20-40 points)
  • Net Promoter Score (NPS): Measures likelihood users will recommend your product
  • Task Success Rate: Percentage of users completing key flows without assistance
  • Time-on-Task: Efficiency improvements in completing core actions
  • Error Rates: Reduction in user mistakes and misclicks
  • Emotional Response: User sentiment analysis through interviews and surveys

Case Example: Enterprise CRM redesign saw these improvements after UX overhaul:

  • SUS score increased from 52 (poor) to 82 (excellent)
  • Average task completion time reduced by 65%
  • Training time for new employees cut from 3 days to 4 hours
  • Customer support tickets related to UI dropped by 78%

Mobile-First Methodology

Our mobile-first approach ensures optimal performance on all devices:

  • Content Prioritization: Forced focus on what matters most to users
  • Touch-Friendly Design: Minimum 48px touch targets, proper spacing
  • Performance Optimization: Lightweight assets and efficient interactions
  • Progressive Enhancement: Core functionality works on all devices, with enhancements for larger screens
  • Adaptive Layouts: Breakpoints based on content needs rather than arbitrary device sizes

Key Statistics:

  • 58% of all website traffic now comes from mobile devices
  • 53% of users abandon sites taking over 3 seconds to load on mobile
  • Mobile-first designs see 25% higher engagement on desktop as well

We design using Chrome DevTools device emulation and test on real devices throughout the process to ensure perfect rendering.

Accessibility Standards

We adhere to WCAG 2.1 AA standards for all designs:

  • Color Contrast: Minimum 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
  • Keyboard Navigation: All functionality available without a mouse
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Proper ARIA labels and semantic HTML
  • Motion Considerations: Options to reduce or disable animations
  • Text Alternatives: Descriptive alt text for all meaningful images
  • Form Accessibility: Clear labels, error messages, and instructions

Why Accessibility Matters:

  • 1 in 4 adults in the US has some form of disability
  • Accessible sites have 50% better SEO performance
  • In many countries, accessibility is legally required
  • Accessible designs improve usability for all users

We conduct automated and manual accessibility audits throughout the design process using tools like axe and WAVE.
